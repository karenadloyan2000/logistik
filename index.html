Full-service dispatching

Full-service dispatching is beyond than just finding you suitable loads, it also takes care of most of the time-consuming paperwork and phone calls involved in hauling freight and enables you to concentrate on the most important part of your business – driving safely and delivering your load timely. Research and Book Loads for You – Your dispatcher will keep detailed records of your equipment, preferred rates, and lanes in order to book you the highest paying loads available every time.

Complete Carrier Packets and Rate Confirmation Paperwork – Once we book your load, Low Coster Dispatching will make sure to complete your carrier packets with your broker and fill out and sign the rate confirmation, saving you time better spent hauling.

Update Broker While in Transit – Your designated dispatcher will keep your broker updated at all times while you are enroute, relieving you of that burden. Billing and Factoring Work After Delivery – When the load has been delivered and you have POD.

We also make sure that your paperwork is quickly and accurately billed to the broker or shipper and submit the invoice for factoring along with all required documents.

FAQ

Does Low Coster Dispatch help with invoicing and how do I get paid?

Your invoicing and collection support is included in our services at NO additional cost. As we provide original Rate Cons, you will get paid directly from Shippers and/or Brokers.

Can I do a self dispatching or work with other dispatch companies in a same time?

As we don’t have any kinds of term contracts or forced dispatch, You have capacity to book loads by yourself, also use other dispatch companies, and of course no one from Low Coster dispatch will charge You for any other loads except the ones, that were completed with our dispatch team.

Is there any other fees except the fee for completed load with Low Coster dispatch?

ABSOLUTELY NO! There is no weekly or monthly payments, no membership fees and of course You’re paying us our fee only after being paid by the broker himself. Our accounting specialists contacting You every time to confirm, that payment from the broker has been released and You are ready to pay us our fee.

Can I skip a load?

Yes! You can skip loads, there are no penalties or fees. The purpose of our service is to provide an exact match to our carriers’ individual preferences without any commitments.

Which loadboards do you use?

We have multiple sources, we have 1 private loadboard, and we use several public networks like Truck Stop and DAT. Our partner brokers send us thousand of e-mail messages with exact loads mainly on daily basis, then the system integrates those messages with this internal loadboard, and that’s when we start our job.

Are there any commitments?

NO! There are absolutely no long or short-term commitments, minimum number of loads, or any obligations. Accounts can be voided with a phone call or e-mail at any time.

Haw do I start getting loads?

As there are no contracts, the only action required is to fill out a “request a call” form on our website and one of our trained onboarding professionals will contact you within 1 business day.
